Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

The short version

Rounder does not collect, store, or share any of your personal information. There are no accounts and no servers. Everything you enter — player names, Venmo handles, buy-ins, and game history — stays on your device and never leaves it, unless you personally choose to share it.

Information we collect

None. Rounder has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, device identifiers, or usage data.

Where your data lives

All the information you enter is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device database. It is not uploaded to us or to anyone else. If you delete the app, that data is removed with it.

Sharing and payments

  • Share: when you tap Share to send a settle-up summary, your device's standard share sheet sends it to whatever app you choose. That is your action, and the content only goes where you send it.
  • Venmo: Rounder does not process payments. When you tap to pay someone, we open Venmo (or your browser) with the payment pre-filled. You confirm the payment inside Venmo. Anything you do in Venmo is governed by Venmo's own privacy policy, not ours.

Third parties

Rounder includes no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking software of any kind. No data is sold or shared, because none is collected.

Children

Rounder is intended for adults and is not directed at children. Because the app collects no data, it collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If Rounder ever adds features that change how information is handled (for example, optional accounts or cloud sync), we will update this policy and the "last updated" date above before those features ship.
